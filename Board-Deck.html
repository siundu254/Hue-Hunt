The Raid Track

Four deed corners · numbered beams to the vault — classic board-game tension

🏁 HQ · space 0Your corner deed — teams rally here like Monopoly GO
★ Bounty · 2 & 5Draw Raid Bounty — bonus space, steal, or wild hunt
⚡ Chaos · 3, 6, 9Spin Chaos Compass — twist, swap, sudden rules
💀 Death · 7Risk losing a space or sit out next raid
👑 Crown · 10First team here places the Raid Crown — wins
🎯 TerritoryHunt in your zone = +1 bonus space (home-turf edge)
  • Blitz round — all teams hunt the same object; first back advances +2 (auction energy)
  • Secret deeds — hidden objectives behind corner screens; complete for a free bounty draw
  • Raid Combo Bonus — three successful Raids in a row → bonus move or Bounty draw; resets on fail
  • Last Stand Bonus — last-place team gets one comeback per game (extra Bounty, Chaos reroll, or double mission)
