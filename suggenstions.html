Raid Mode Deck — one box, four age bands

Raid Mode Deck (HHB-MODE-24) · 24 cards · 63×88mm — matches mission brief cards · Tuck box fits vault well beside Bounty + Chaos. Not every innovation is a card — ceremony rules and venue modifiers stay on the overlay.

  1. 1
  2. Look at the table — pick ONE age band (green / orange / purple / gold)
  3. 2
  4. Shuffle only that band (6 cards)
  5. 3
  6. Draw ONE mode at game start — OR draw when any team hits ⚡
  7. 4
  8. Play that mode for one round (or whole game if marked).

Mixed ages: Youngest player sets the band — OR facilitator splits: green modes for rounds 1–3, one orange finale if everyone is 13+.

Not in deck: Who Raided? ceremony · Crown Snap · venue rule modifiers (on overlay) · Seasonal Breach packs (separate SKU)

🟢 Family Raid · 6+

Home · School · Community · mixed-age tables

🔗 Combo Crew — Three successful raids in a row → that team moves +1 (once per game).
🧩 Mashup Map — Same rules, silly geography — Kitchen + Gym + Library + Patio.
🔄 Hide & Seek Raid — Team hides an object in their zone; rivals get one guess — wrong guess = no penalty, just laughs.
📣 Cheer Squad — Each spectator may shout ONE hint to one team during a raid.
🌈 Wild Zone — Any room counts as home turf for this mission only.
💎 Sticker Relic — First team to space 5 earns a passport stamp — +1 on their next Bounty draw.

🟠 Party Raid · 13+

Party · Gym · Retail · teen birthdays

💥 Vault Breach — Everyone hunts the same object; first back +2, second +1, others +0.
🔥 Heat Crucible — Each confirmed raid shaves 5s off the timer for the rest of the game.
⚡ Blitz Round — First team back with it advances +2; no one else moves.
🌑 Blackout Brief — Flip card face-down — no repeats during the raid.
👑 Crown Heist — Team at space 9 may challenge leader at 10 with a DUEL card; winner crowns.
🛡️ Last Stand — Once per game: draw 2 Bounties OR reroll Chaos OR move +2.

🟣 Expert Raid · Adults

Office · Museum · Library · Airport · HR retreats

📡 Echo Mission — Find something that rhymes with, matches texture of, or lives near last game's winning object → +1.
⚖️ Rival Warden — Once per round Warden vetoes one beam advance — must justify in character.
🎰 Spectator Syndicate — Wager on which team finishes the raid first; winners play their card.
🤫 Silent Gallery — No talking from Brief until Reveal — mime and point only.
🔗 Expert Combo — Three wins in a row → choose Relic stamp, Bounty, or +2 (not all three).
🃏 Facilitator Wild — Play that mode this round — expert table chose the chaos.

💛 Gentle Raid · 55+ / Care

Care · Sunrise · hospital wellness · memory cafés

🛋️ Seated Raid — Every object must be reachable from a chair — facilitator approves.
📖 Story Object — Find something that sparks a 30-second memory; group votes favourite.
🤝 Buddy Raid — Two corners share one find — both advance +1 on success.
🌻 Slow Crucible — Gentle band only: extend timer by 10s for the next two raids.
💛 Warm Bounty — Bounty draws give compliments or +1 only — no steals.
🎖️ Gentle Stamp — Every confirmed raid earns a stamp — five stamps = group photo at crown.

Outside the box — innovation playbook

Not incremental tweaks — modes that flip the scavenger loop, weaponize the passport, and turn spectators into bettors. Mix one per game night; stack two for chaos.

Raid Track twists

🔥 Heat Crucible
The room gets faster every round.

Each confirmed raid shaves 5s off the printed timer for the rest of the game. By space 8 the whole table is sprinting.

Escalating panic without changing the board — pure facilitator dial.

🔗 Raid Combo Streak
Momentum rewards consistency.

Three successful raids in a row → choose +1 beam, draw Bounty, or stamp a Relic on the Raid Passport. One failed reveal resets the chain.

Comeback arcs feel earned; streaks create table-wide drama.

🛡️ Last Stand
Nobody checks out early.

Last-place team gets one comeback per game: Crown Heist duel, flood of Bounty draws, or reroll a Chaos outcome.

Space-10 finishes stay competitive even when someone was stuck at 3.

👑 Crown Heist
Steal the finish line.

Team at space 9 may challenge the leader at 10 to a DUEL card. Winner takes the crown attempt this round.

Monopoly-style auction energy on the final spaces.

Mission & hunt modes

🔄 Reverse Raid
Hide instead of hunt.

Teams stash an object in their zone. Rivals get one guess each — wrong guess costs a beam space.

Flips the scavenger loop; hilarious in offices and living rooms.

💥 Vault Breach
Chaos goes table-wide.

When any team lands on ⚡, ALL teams hunt the same object simultaneously. First back gets +2; everyone else +0 or −1.

Sudden shared moment — like a Monopoly tax on the whole table.

🌑 Blackout Brief
Read it once. Remember it.

Captain reads the mission card aloud once, then flips it face-down. No repeats during the raid.

Memory pressure without new components — expert mode in one sentence.

📡 Echo Mission
Your last raid haunts this one.

App QR recalls the winning object from a prior session. Find something that rhymes, matches texture, or lives in the same zone → bonus space.

Board game and app become one long-running heist saga.

Table & spectator play

🎰 Spectator Syndicate
Guests bet on the chaos.

Non-playing guests each wager a Bounty card on which team finishes the raid first. Winners keep the card's effect.

Turns wallflowers into loud cheerleaders — great for parties and conferences.

⚖️ Rival Warden
A fifth player with a gavel.

Rotating neutral player holds the Warden gavel. Once per round they can veto one team's beam advance — must justify it in character.

Built-in referee for HR retreats and mixed-age tables.

Cross-session & SKU magic

🧩 Mashup Map
Impossible geography.

Mix corners from two overlays — Office break room + Museum gallery on one board. Same rules, surreal floor plan.

Infinite novelty from existing SKUs; no new board required.

💎 Raid Relics
Passport stamps that change the next game.

Magnetic chips slot into the Raid Passport: Reroll Chaos, Whisper Immunity, +1 on first Bounty. Earned, not bought.

Legacy progression across game nights — collectible without loot-box vibes.

🎃 Seasonal Breach
Overlays that break their own rules.

Halloween pack swaps Chaos for Curse deck; Party pack adds dance-spin compass faces; Space Raid = lights-off blackout brief by default.

Seasonal SKUs feel like events, not reskins.

Win moment & components

📣 Who Raided?
The victory call-and-response.

Facilitator: "Who Raided?" Table: "{Team} Raided!" Crown snaps on pedestal. Photo. Done.

Brand catchphrase baked into the win moment — not an afterthought.

🧲 Crown Snap
Magnetic pedestal payoff.

Hidden magnet in crown well — crown clicks home with a satisfying thunk. Optional fanfare chip in premium box.

Tactile Instagram moment every single game.
